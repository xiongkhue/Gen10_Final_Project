About The Arch

Food as it should be. Food should taste good. It should feel good. It should do good things for you and the world around you. At The Arch, our food, service, and modern twist on some of the biggest cuisines will be the gateway to a one of a kind experience for you and your guests. Japanese burgers and bbq, Northeast American Coast Southeast Asian seafood, or French Australian pasta and pastries, whichever palet you have it's gaurenteed to be expanded with your experience here. Our service is the best you'll find anywhere, we employ only the best cooks and hosts from our private 4 year intensive training program. Once inside you'll find an atmosphere that is both welcoming yet exciting and fierce. This will allow you to comfortably enjoy your time with your guest while waiting in intense antcipation for your food. Our interior is classic yet very modern and while waiting you and your guest can enjoy performances with classic instruments nearby the open area. While it sounds like we put in a lot of work into our serviecs it is no where near the amount we put into our food. From the plating, smells, texture, and taste, The Arch you'll find is a newfound gateway to the world of food and service.
